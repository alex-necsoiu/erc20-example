# Unlockd protocol
Official Golang implementation of the Unlockd protocol.

[![Discord](https://img.shields.io/badge/discord-join%20chat-blue.svg)](https://discord.gg/vj3yjBz6)

Our lending & borrowing market allows DeFi, Art, Real-World and Gaming users to compound their wealth with an NFT-backed, cross-chain and instant loans. 
Back End infrastructure that interacts with Unlockd Protocols Smart Contracts and implements business logic. [Based on Go Kit architecture](https://shijuvar.medium.com/go-microservices-with-go-kit-introduction-43a757398183).
